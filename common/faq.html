Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Which classes and boards do you cover? ⌄
We provide coaching for 8th to 12th Science students covering CBSE, ICSE, and Maharashtra State Board with complete academic support.
Do you prepare students for JEE, NEET, and MHT-CET? ⌄
Yes, our program is designed to balance board exams along with competitive exams like JEE, NEET, and MHT-CET through concept-based learning and regular testing.
How is Elementrix Academy different from other classes? ⌄
Unlike crowded coaching classes, we focus on small batches, personal attention, and continuous performance tracking. Every student gets individual guidance to improve weak areas.
Will my child get personal attention? ⌄
Yes. We keep limited students per batch so that every student receives proper guidance, doubt-solving, and mentorship.
How do you track student progress? ⌄
We conduct regular tests, provide performance reports, and also update parents about their child's progress through feedback sessions.
Do you communicate with parents regularly? ⌄
Yes, we conduct parent-teacher interactions and share updates about performance, attendance, and improvement areas.
What if a student misses a class? ⌄
We provide support materials and guidance to cover missed topics so students don’t fall behind.
Do you provide study material and notes? ⌄
Yes, we provide written notes, assignments, and test papers for complete preparation.
How can I take admission? ⌄
You can visit our center or contact us via call/WhatsApp to book your seat. Limited seats are available in each batch.
Do you offer demo lectures? ⌄
Yes, we offer free demo lectures so students can experience our teaching before enrolling.
Is there any discount for early admission? ⌄
Yes, early joiners can avail special discounts and secure their seat at the best price.
